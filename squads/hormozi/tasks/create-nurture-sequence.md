## Purpose
Create a lead nurture sequence using the 4 Pillars: Availability, Speed, Personalization, Volume.

> "If they don't show, they can't buy. Nurture is about making sure they show." — Alex Hormozi

---

## The 4 Pillars of Lead Nurture

### Pillar 1: AVAILABILITY
Be reachable through multiple channels.
- Phone
- Email
- SMS
- Social DM
- Chat

**Rule:** The more ways they can reach you, the more likely they will.

### Pillar 2: SPEED
Respond faster than anyone else.
- Under 5 minutes for new leads
- Under 1 hour for existing conversations
- Under 24 hours for all inquiries

**Data:** Leads contacted within 5 minutes are 21x more likely to convert.

### Pillar 3: PERSONALIZATION
Make them feel like the only customer.
- Use their name
- Reference their situation
- Customize recommendations

**Rule:** Generic = ignored. Personal = engaged.

### Pillar 4: VOLUME
More touches = more chances.
- Multiple touchpoints per day
- Multiple channels
- Keep going until they buy or die

**Rule:** Most people give up too early. Persistence wins.

---

## Nurture Sequence Architecture

### Day 0: Lead Captured
- Immediate: Automated confirmation
- Within 5 min: Personal outreach
- Within 1 hour: Follow-up if no response

### Days 1-3: Hot Lead Phase
- 2-3 touches per day
- Mix of channels (call, text, email)
- Value-focused messages

### Days 4-7: Warm Lead Phase
- 1-2 touches per day
- Case studies and social proof
- Soft offers to engage

### Days 8-14: Nurture Phase
- 1 touch per day
- Educational content
- Objection handling

### Days 15-30: Long-term Nurture
- 2-3 touches per week
- Newsletter/content inclusion
- Periodic direct offers

### Days 30+: Reactivation Loop
- Monthly check-ins
- New offers
- Event invitations

## Touchpoint Templates

### Text Message (Day 0, 5 min)
```
Hey [Name], this is [You] from [Company].
I saw you [signed up/requested info].
Quick question: what's your #1 goal with [topic]?
```

### Call Script (Day 0, No Answer)
```
"Hey [Name], this is [You]. I'm following up on
your interest in [topic]. I'd love to help you
[achieve result]. Call me back at [number] or
just reply to the text I sent. Talk soon."
```

### Email (Day 1)
```
Subject: Quick question, [Name]

Hey [Name],

I noticed you [action they took] and wanted
to personally reach out.

What specifically are you trying to accomplish?

Once I know, I can point you in the right direction.

[Signature]
```

### Value Touch (Day 3)
```
Subject: This might help

[Name],

I put together [resource] for people trying to
[achieve result].

Thought you might find it useful: [link]

Let me know if you have any questions.

[Signature]
```

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Map the Journey
What happens from lead capture to sale?

### Step 2: Design Touchpoints
Create 15-20 touchpoints across the journey.

### Step 3: Write All Copy
Scripts, emails, texts for each touchpoint.

### Step 4: Set Up Automation
Configure sequences in your CRM.

### Step 5: Train Team on Speed
Everyone responds within 5 minutes.

### Step 6: Track and Optimize
Measure show rates, response rates, conversions.
